# Confluence / Wiki Structure (Enterprise)

## Space Governance
- Space Owner: `PMO`
- Content Owner per section wajib ditetapkan.
- Review cadence: monthly for operational docs, quarterly for architecture docs.

## Top-Level Structure
1. Product Governance (BRD/PRD/Roadmap/KPI)
2. Engineering Architecture (HLD/LLD/TSD/API/DB)
3. Delivery Management (Sprint, release, change log)
4. Quality Assurance (test assets, RTM, UAT)
5. Operations & Incident (runbooks, postmortems)
6. Compliance & Audit (controls, evidence, approvals)
7. Onboarding & Training

## Documentation Standards
- Template mandatory
- Last reviewed date mandatory
- Broken-link check every sprint
