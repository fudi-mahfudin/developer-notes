## Strategic Themes
1. Access to care (booking and teleconsult adoption)
2. Clinical operations efficiency (admin workload reduction)
3. Reliability and compliance (auditability, uptime, security)

## 12-Month Roadmap
### Q1 - Core Access
- Booking v2, reminder orchestration, doctor schedule validation.
- KPI target: digital booking ratio >= 65%.

### Q2 - Virtual Care
- Teleconsult hardening, fallback channel, consent workflow.
- KPI target: teleconsult completion >= 90%.

### Q3 - Interoperability
- HIS/LIS integration pilot, SSO for hospital staff.
- KPI target: 2 enterprise customers live.

### Q4 - Governance & Scale
- DR simulation, audit evidence automation, performance optimization.
- KPI target: uptime >= 99.9%.
